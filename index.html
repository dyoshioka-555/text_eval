Text style transfer Evaluation Test

Thank you for your cooperation in my experiment.
This experiment is evaluating test of the style transfered text with Likert scale.
Please read both original text and the automatically generated potential rewrite of the text and complete the following ratings.
I will explain the experimental procedure.

Experimental Procedure

  1. Enter your name and select the set list number from 1 to 10.
  2. Press "Start Experiment" button.
  3. Read to the two text, original text and generated text, and give it the score you think it deserves.
    In this evaluation experiment, you will be asked to evaluate the generated text from three perspectives:"degree of style transformation", "content preservation", and "fluency".
  4. Press "next" button and start next evaluation.
  5. When you finish this experiment, press "finish" button and download the result.
    Send it to slack or the following email address.
  6. Email address: yoshioka.daiki@g.sp.m.is.nagoya-u.ac.jp

Notes

  • This experiment takes about 30 minutes.
  • Please do not update the page during this experiment.
  • You may not be able to download the results on your phone, so please use your PC.
